Keepsakes

Australians and The Great War

To mark the centenary of the First World War, Keepsakes explored the diaries, photographs, letters and mementos kept and treasured as reminders of the Great War.

Keepsakes was on display at the National Library of Australia from 26 November 2014 to 19 July 2015.

This site displays content from Trove related to the themes of the exhibition. It is a demonstration of how resources collected using Trove lists can be reused through the Trove API to create new interfaces and applications.
